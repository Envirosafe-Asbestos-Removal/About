Asbestos Surveys & Testing

If your property was built before the year 2000, it may contain asbestos. Our professional surveys identify the type, location and condition of any asbestos‑containing materials (ACMs) so you can manage them safely.

We offer management surveys, refurbishment/demolition surveys and sampling services. Samples are analysed by UKAS‑accredited laboratories to confirm whether asbestos is present. Results may show that ACMs can remain in place with regular monitoring rather than removal.

Why Conduct an Asbestos Survey?

  • Ensure compliance with the Control of Asbestos Regulations 2012
  • Protect building occupants and contractors from exposure
  • Identify ACMs before renovation or demolition work
  • Plan appropriate removal, encapsulation or management strategies
