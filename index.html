Why Choose Us?

We are HSE licensed and BOHS-accredited with years of experience. Our team ensures safe, compliant removal of asbestos from residential, commercial and industrial premises. We offer quick turnaround, competitive rates and fully trained operatives.

Service Areas

We serve London, South East England and surrounding areas. From major boroughs like Camden, Westminster and Croydon to towns across Surrey, Kent, Sussex and Essex.

Comprehensive Asbestos Solutions

When it comes to protecting your property and the people who use it, choosing an experienced asbestos removal specialist is essential. Envirosafe Removal Specialists provides a full range of services, from initial surveys and testing to the removal, encapsulation and safe disposal of hazardous materials. Our experts understand the unique challenges posed by older structures as well as modern buildings that may still contain legacy asbestos. We invest in state‑of‑the‑art equipment, continuous training and rigorous risk management to deliver results you can trust.

Based in Hampshire, we work throughout London and the South East, helping homeowners, landlords, commercial property managers and public bodies manage asbestos responsibly. Our approach prioritises clear communication and transparency: we explain your legal obligations, outline the options available and tailor our service to your budget and timeline. Whether you need urgent remediation after discovering damaged insulation or a management survey before starting renovations, our team is ready to guide you every step of the way.
