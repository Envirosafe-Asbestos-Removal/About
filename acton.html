About Asbestos in Acton

Acton and its surrounding areas feature a diverse range of residences, from mid‑century family homes to modern apartments. Our team is skilled at identifying and removing hidden fibrous asbestos from lofts, soffits, pipe lagging and garage roofs. We work discreetly and efficiently, leaving your property clean and ready for renovation or sale.

Within Acton, there are many heritage properties that require special care. Our operatives are trained to carry out asbestos abatement in delicate environments, ensuring listed cornices, mouldings and timber frames remain intact while eliminating asbestos risks.

Beyond the town centre, the Acton region encompasses agricultural buildings, barns and stables. We specialise in removing corrugated cement roofing, water tanks and bitumen sheeting from these agricultural structures so you can repurpose them safely.

As a hub for shops, Acton has a variety of factories. We provide tailored solutions for office blocks, retail outlets and industrial estates, including site surveys, encapsulation and licensed removal when required.

Health and safety is at the heart of everything we do. We adhere to UK legislation, including the Control of Asbestos Regulations 2012, and provide method statements, risk assessments and waste consignment notes for every job. Our ongoing training ensures we use the latest techniques and equipment to manage asbestos materials safely.

We are proud to be part of the community in and around Acton. All waste is taken to licensed facilities to minimise environmental impact, and we actively raise awareness about the dangers of asbestos through local events and partnerships.
