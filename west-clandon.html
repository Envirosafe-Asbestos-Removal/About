About Asbestos in West Clandon

West Clandon and its surrounding areas feature a diverse range of properties, from mid‑century family homes to modern apartments. Our team is skilled at identifying and removing hidden asbestos materials from lofts, soffits, pipe lagging and garage roofs. We work discreetly and efficiently, leaving your property clean and ready for renovation or sale.

Within West Clandon, there are many older properties that require special care. Our operatives are trained to carry out asbestos remediation in delicate environments, ensuring listed cornices, mouldings and timber frames remain intact while eliminating asbestos risks.

Beyond the town centre, the West Clandon region encompasses rural properties, barns and stables. We specialise in removing corrugated cement roofing, water tanks and bitumen sheeting from these agricultural structures so you can repurpose them safely.

As a hub for commercial enterprises, West Clandon has a variety of industrial units. We provide tailored solutions for office blocks, retail outlets and industrial estates, including site surveys, encapsulation and licensed removal when required.

Health and safety is at the heart of everything we do. We adhere to UK legislation, including the Control of Asbestos Regulations 2012, and provide method statements, risk assessments and waste consignment notes for every job. Our ongoing training ensures we use the latest techniques and equipment to manage hazardous asbestos safely.

We are proud to be part of the community in and around West Clandon. All waste is taken to licensed facilities to minimise environmental impact, and we actively raise awareness about the dangers of asbestos through local events and partnerships.
