About Us

Envirosafe Removal Specialists is a trusted asbestos removal company serving London and South East England. With over 15 years of experience, we specialise in both licensed and non‑licensed removal, surveys, encapsulation and disposal. Our team is HSE‑licensed, BOHS‑accredited and fully trained in safe working practices.

We pride ourselves on fast turnaround times, competitive rates and a personal approach. From small domestic projects to major commercial contracts, we treat every job with the same dedication to safety and quality.

Our Certifications & Accreditations

  • HSE Licensed Asbestos Removal Contractor
  • BOHS P402/P405 Qualified Surveyors
  • Compliance with Control of Asbestos Regulations 2012

Our mission is to provide a complete asbestos solution with minimal disruption and maximum peace of mind.

Our Story

Envirosafe Removal Specialists was founded by industry veterans who recognised a need for a more personal and accountable approach to asbestos remediation. Starting as a small team serving local homeowners, we have grown into a reputable contractor covering the entire South East. Over the years we have tackled projects of every size and complexity, from domestic garage roof removals to large‑scale commercial refurbishments. Despite our growth we remain committed to our original vision: to deliver safe, compliant and customer‑focused services that protect people and the environment.

Our success is built on long‑term relationships with clients, regulatory bodies and fellow professionals. We invest in the latest equipment, training and technology to stay ahead of industry developments. This forward‑thinking approach means we can adapt quickly to changing regulations and offer innovative solutions, while still delivering the personal service our clients expect.

Our Team & Values

We believe that our people are our greatest asset. Every member of our workforce, from surveyors and removal operatives to project managers and support staff, shares a commitment to excellence and continuous improvement. We foster a culture of safety, integrity and respect, ensuring that everyone goes home safely at the end of each day. Regular training, mentoring and performance reviews help us maintain high standards and nurture talent from within.

As a responsible contractor, we are also dedicated to sustainability and community engagement. We follow best practices for waste management, minimise environmental impact wherever possible, and support local initiatives that promote health and wellbeing. By choosing Envirosafe Removal Specialists, you are supporting a company that values people, professionalism and the planet.
