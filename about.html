About Us

Envirosafe Removal is a trusted asbestos removal company serving London and South East England. With over 15 years of experience, we specialise in both licensed and non‑licensed removal, surveys, encapsulation and disposal. Our team is HSE‑licensed, BOHS‑accredited and fully trained in safe working practices.

We pride ourselves on fast turnaround times, competitive rates and a personal approach. From small domestic projects to major commercial contracts, we treat every job with the same dedication to safety and quality.

Our Certifications & Accreditations

  • HSE Licensed Asbestos Removal Contractor
  • BOHS P402/P405 Qualified Surveyors
  • Compliance with Control of Asbestos Regulations 2012

Our mission is to provide a complete asbestos solution with minimal disruption and maximum peace of mind.
