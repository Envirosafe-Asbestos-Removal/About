Non-Licensed Asbestos Removal

Certain asbestos work can be carried out without an HSE licence because the risk of fibre release is lower. To qualify, the work must be sporadic and of low intensity, with exposure less than 0.6 fibres per cubic centimetre of air over a 10‑minute period and not exceed 0.1 fibres per cubic centimetre averaged over a 4‑hour period. Examples include removing asbestos cement sheets, drilling small holes in textured coatings or encapsulating asbestos.

Even without a licence, non-licensed work still requires trained personnel, proper risk assessment and safe working procedures. Our team is fully trained to carry out these tasks and will advise whether your project falls under licensed or non-licensed requirements.

Examples of Non-Licensed Work

  • Removing asbestos cement roofing and guttering in good condition
  • Drilling or cutting small holes in textured coatings
  • Encapsulating asbestos‑containing materials with sealant
  • Cleaning up small pieces of asbestos debris
  • Air monitoring and sampling tasks

Contact us to discuss your project – we can determine whether the work is licensable and ensure it is completed safely.

Our Approach to Non‑Licensed Work

Non‑licensed tasks may present lower exposure risks, but they still demand careful controls and experienced operatives. Before starting any job we carry out a risk assessment to confirm that exposures will remain below the Control of Asbestos Regulations 2012 thresholds. We also ensure that workers are trained in safe handling techniques, personal protective equipment is worn, and work areas are cleaned and inspected on completion. Materials are wetted down where appropriate to suppress dust and removed intact wherever possible.

Clients appreciate our professionalism and attention to detail, even on small projects. We document the procedures used, provide you with copies of risk assessments and method statements, and advise you on ongoing management of any remaining asbestos. By engaging a competent contractor for non‑licensed work, you reduce liabilities and gain assurance that the job has been done correctly.
