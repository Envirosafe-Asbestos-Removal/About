Canary Wharf and its surrounding areas feature a diverse range of houses, from mid‑century family homes to modern apartments. Our team is skilled at identifying and removing hidden harmful asbestos from lofts, soffits, pipe lagging and garage roofs. Our operatives are trained to carry out removal of hazardous materials in delicate environments, ensuring listed cornices, mouldings and timber frames remain intact. Beyond the town centre, the Canary Wharf region encompasses agricultural buildings, barns and stables. We specialise in removing corrugated cement roofing, water tanks and bitumen sheeting from agricultural structures, so you can repurpose them safely. Our ongoing training ensures we use the latest techniques and equipment to manage asbestos materials safely. We are proud to be part of the community in and around Canary Wharf.
